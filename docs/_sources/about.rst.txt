About
=====

Michael is currently a core library developer at Crunch.io, a subsidiary of YouGov. His software development experience includes full-stack web application development, data analysis and visualization, and computational modeling/scientific computing. He previous position was as a research assistant at the University of Missouri-Kansas City, where his work in nanophotonics has been featured and published in several of the top journals for chemistry, physics, and materials science. He is a practiced orator, having participated as an oral presenter for the American Chemical Society at both Regional Conferences and the National Expo. He is a driven individual who possesses the desire to take on the greatest of challenges with logical and intelligent approach.
